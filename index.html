MIKE TYSON

For other people named Mike Tyson, see Mike Tyson (disambiguation).

Michael Gerard Tyson (born June 30, 1966) is an American former professional boxer
who competed between 1985 and 2024. Nicknamed "Iron Mike"[4] and "Kid Dynamite"
in his early career, and later known as "the Baddest Man on the Planet",[5] Tyson is
regarded as one of the greatest heavyweight boxers of all time.[6] He reigned as the
undisputed world heavyweight champion from 1987 to 1990.
